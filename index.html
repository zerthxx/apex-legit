Join LEGIT Clan

We're building a serious long-term competitive roster — grinding ranked to high Predator, competing in scrims daily, and working toward Challenger Circuit and semi-pro level. If you're committed, this is your team.

What We Offer
⚔️

Daily Scrims

Organized daily scrim sessions against competitive teams to sharpen every aspect of your game.

💎

High Predator Grind

Not just reaching Predator — maintaining a high Predator placement every season through focused ranked grinding.

🎬

VOD Reviews

Regular gameplay review sessions to break down mistakes, refine macro, and accelerate individual improvement.

🏆

Community Tournaments

Active participation in community cups and tournaments building a strong competitive track record.

🤝

Team Chemistry

Positive, drama-free environment built on trust, communication, and a shared drive to improve.

🚀

Long-Term Development

We're building something lasting. A permanent roster with ambitions beyond ranked — toward ALGS and beyond.

Who We're Looking For
🎮 IGL

Experienced in-game leader with strong macro understanding, confident end-game calling, and the ability to make decisive calls under pressure.

💀 Fragger

Mechanical players who can confidently take fights, create openings for the team, and consistently perform in high-pressure ranked and scrim environments.

🛡️ Anchor / Refrag

Players with excellent positioning, teamfight awareness, and the ability to support engagements and clean up after the fragger opens fights.

18+ PC Only Daily Availability Scrim Commitment Strong Communication Competitive Mindset VOD Review Willingness No Team-Hopping
